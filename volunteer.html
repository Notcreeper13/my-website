Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum age to volunteer?

The minimum age varies by program. Most opportunities require volunteers to be at least 16 years old.

Do I need previous experience?

While some roles require specific experience, many don't. We provide training for most positions.

How long do I need to commit?

Commitment periods vary by role. Some opportunities are one-time events, while others require longer commitments.

Can I volunteer as part of a group?

Yes! We welcome both individual and group volunteers. Contact us for group opportunities.
